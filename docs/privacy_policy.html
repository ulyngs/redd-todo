Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2025

Thank you for using ReDD Todo ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We believe in your privacy. ReDD Todo does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or usage information to us or any third parties.

The application is designed to operate entirely locally on your device. All data you enter into ReDD Todo remains on your local machine.

2. Basecamp Integration

If you choose to connect ReDD Todo to your Basecamp account:

  • The connection is used solely to fetch your Basecamp tasks and display them within the application.
  • Authentication tokens and data retrieved from Basecamp are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not have access to your Basecamp credentials or data.
  • No information from your Basecamp account is transmitted to us or any other servers.

3. Data Storage

All data created or processed by ReDD Todo is stored locally on your computer's hard drive. You have full control over this data.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
